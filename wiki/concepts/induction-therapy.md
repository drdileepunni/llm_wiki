# Induction Therapy

## Definition
The initial phase of immunosuppressive treatment aimed at rapidly achieving disease remission in systemic vasculitis.

## In ANCA-Associated Vasculitis
The goal is to suppress disease activity quickly to prevent organ damage, particularly irreversible renal scarring.

### Current Standards
- **Preferred agent**: [[Rituximab]] (first-line)
- **Alternative**: [[Cyclophosphamide]] (for severe disease or rituximab contraindications)
- **Corticosteroids**: High-dose IV methylprednisolone followed by oral prednisone taper

### Duration
Typically 3-6 months until remission achieved
